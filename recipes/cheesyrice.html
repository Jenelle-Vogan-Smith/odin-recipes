Cheesy Rice Recipe




Description

Quick & easy meal that's both tasty and hardy and easily adjusted to serve more people.

Ingredients

  • 1 Hillshire Farm Smoked Sausage
  • 1 package Knorr Spanish Rice
    • 2 cups water
    • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 can Green Giant Mexicorn
  • 1/2 white onion
  • 1/2 green bellpepper
  • 1 cup shredded Mexican blend cheese

Steps

  1. Open rice and pour into pot.
  2. Add water, butter, Mexicorn & diced sausage, onion and bellpepper.
  3. Put pot on stove and bring to a boil.
  4. Once it's boiling, reduce heat to a simmer and cover for 12-15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Remove from heat and mix in the cheese
  6. Enjoy!
