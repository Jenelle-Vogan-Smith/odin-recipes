Kale Chips Recipe




Description

Healthy "chips" made with kale and sesame seeds. These are a low calorie nutritious snack!

Ingredients

  • Kale (chopped)
  • Coconut oil
  • Salt
  • Toasted sesame seeds

Steps

  1. Preheat over to 300 degrees.
  2. In a bowl, toss kale and coconut oil together. (You may need to slightly heat the oil first in order to liquify it.)
  3. Place on lined baking sheet.
  4. Bake until crisp (about 20 minutes)
  5. Let cool.
  6. Serve & enjoy!
